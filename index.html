OT bars are unique and tasty granola bars made with vegan and gluten free ingredients. They are hand crafted by two borthers in Philadelphia in an effort to provide healthy and neutrional snacks at an affordable price, while combating hunger in the Philly area. 10% of profits are donated to Philabundance.
